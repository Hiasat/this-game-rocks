This Game Rocks!

Game Instructions:

  • The goal of the game is to move the rocks to the buttom level of the
    graph ( purple nodes). The last player to move is the winner.
  • You will take turns against computer moving one rock in each turn.
  • To move a rock, click on the node that has the rock, then click on the
    node you want the rock to move to.
  • To change the node you chose,click on the same node again.
  • You can only move a rock to its neighboring nodes.
